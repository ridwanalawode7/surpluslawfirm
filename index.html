About
Surplus Law Firm was founded by Barrister Opeyemi Opoola (J.S.D.) in 2027 in Nigeria. It has since grown to what is now an international Law Firm offering services in 195 different Countries. Surplus Law Firm offers legal advise in all known aspects of law and has lawyers who specialise in prosecution and defence. Surplus Law Firm has a staggering 99.99% win rate in over 10,000 court cases. Surplus Law Firm has won numerous accolades as well as awards in the field of law including an Humanitarian Award by the United Nations for upholding of Human Rights and a Nobel Peace Price.
The CEO
Opoola Opeyemi was born on the 28th Februrary 2004. He attened Adeola Model School and Adeola College for his primary and secondary education respectively. He went further to study Law in the University Of Ilorin in Kwara State Nigeria and attended the Lagos Law School in Lagos, Nigeria. He is the founder and current CEO of Surplus Law Firm.
